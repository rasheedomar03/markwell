Wearmill · Mark Exploration · 5 Concepts
01
WM Monogram
W and M sharing one horizontal axis. Both initials. One mark. No decoration.
02
Thread Sweep
Thread pulled into a W and released. Curved, calligraphic, alive. One line — beginning to end.
03
Spool Mark
Two rings. One thread. The spool abstracted to its essential geometry. Timeless.
04
Negative Band
W defined by absence. The mark is what's removed from gold — a printing metaphor.
05
Diamond Mark
W inside a rotated square. Geometric precision meets textile heritage. Works as a seal or emboss.
