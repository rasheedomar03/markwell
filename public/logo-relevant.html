Squeegee

Thread Spool

The face of a rubber stamp — M raised in relief, ready to imprint. "Marking" in its most literal form.

Woven Label
The inside tag of every garment — the final mark of brand ownership. Woven labels are a Markwell product.

Stencil M
M as a stencil cut-out, with ink overspray at edges. Spray paint stenciling is a key decoration method.
